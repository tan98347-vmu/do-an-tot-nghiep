HUONG DAN SU DUNG HE THONG AI VAN BAN

1. GIOI THIEU CHUNG

He thong AI Van Ban la ung dung web ho tro:
- Quan ly mau van ban
- Sinh van ban bang AI tu mau
- Dien bien tu ho so, ngu canh cong ty, tep PDF
- Quan ly van ban da tao
- Chia se, phe duyet, luu tru, yeu thich
- Chat AI va hoi dap tren ngu canh van ban
- Quan tri nguoi dung, nhom, cau hinh AI va sao luu

He thong co 3 nhom doi tuong chinh:
- Nguoi dung thong thuong
- Truong nhom
- Quan tri vien


2. DANG NHAP VA BAT DAU

2.1. Dang nhap
- Mo trang dang nhap cua he thong
- Nhap ten dang nhap va mat khau
- Neu he thong co bat dang nhap xa hoi, ban co the dang nhap bang Google hoac Facebook

2.2. Dang ky
- Neu duoc cap quyen dang ky, vao trang Dang ky
- Dien thong tin tai khoan
- Dang nhap sau khi tao tai khoan thanh cong

2.3. Giao dien tong quan
Sau khi dang nhap, ban se thay menu ben trai, gom cac nhom chuc nang:
- Bang dieu khien
- Van ban
- Mau van ban
- Quan ly van ban
- Prompts
- Ho so ca nhan
- Cho phe duyet
- Quan tri


3. BANG DIEU KHIEN

Bang dieu khien giup ban:
- Xem thong ke nhanh
- Xem mau van ban gan day
- Xem van ban gan day
- Di nhanh den cac chuc nang chinh:
  + Sinh van ban bang AI
  + Hoi dap van ban voi AI
  + Quan ly mau van ban
  + Quan ly van ban
  + Chat AI
  + Ho so ca nhan


4. MAU VAN BAN

4.1. Cac nhom mau van ban
Trong menu "Mau van ban", he thong chia thanh:
- Tao mau van ban
- Upload hang loat
- Mau thong thuong
- Mau phong ban cua toi
- Rieng cua toi
- Yeu thich
- Tat ca (Admin)

Y nghia:
- Mau thong thuong: mau cong khai da duoc phe duyet
- Mau phong ban cua toi: mau chia se theo nhom/phong ban da duoc phe duyet
- Rieng cua toi: mau private cua chinh ban
- Yeu thich: mau ban da danh dau sao

4.2. Tao mau van ban moi
Vao "Tao mau van ban" de tao mau theo 2 cach:
- Nhap noi dung thu cong
- Upload file DOCX roi de AI detect bien

Thong tin thuong dung khi tao/chinh sua mau:
- Ten mau
- Mo ta
- Noi dung mau
- Tag
- Ngay hieu luc
- Ngay het han
- Pham vi hien thi
- Nhom/phong ban dich neu chia se theo phong ban

4.3. Bien trong mau
He thong dung bien theo dang:
{{ten_bien}}

Vi du:
{{ho_ten}}
{{ngay_sinh}}
{{chuc_danh}}

Khi sinh van ban, he thong se thay cac bien nay bang gia tri ban nhap hoac AI dien.

4.4. Upload DOCX va AI detect bien
Neu ban co file Word san:
- Vao Tao mau van ban
- Chon upload DOCX
- Bat AI detect bien
- He thong doc file, nhan dien cac bien, dua noi dung vao editor

Luu y:
- File lon hoac model AI cham co the mat nhieu thoi gian
- He thong da dat timeout rieng dai hon cho cac request AI

4.5. Pham vi hien thi cua mau
Co 3 muc:
- Rieng tu
- Phong ban
- Cong khai

Nghiep vu phe duyet:
- Rieng tu: duoc dung ngay
- Phong ban: neu ban la truong nhom hoac admin thi duoc duyet ngay; neu khong se cho truong nhom duyet
- Cong khai: admin duyet

4.6. Quy tac chon nhom khi chia se mau
- Neu chon "Phong ban", bat buoc phai chon nhom/phong ban dich
- Chi duoc chon nhung nhom ma tai khoan hien tai dang la thanh vien

4.7. Tim kiem, loc, yeu thich, hien thi
Man danh sach mau co:
- O tim kiem
- Bo loc nang cao
- Kieu hien thi:
  + Dang card hien tai
  + Dang danh sach doc
- Checkbox de chon nhieu mau
- Xoa hang loat neu ban co quyen

Tim kiem tren mau ho tro:
- Tim theo ten
- Tim theo mo ta
- Tim theo tag
- Tim theo category
- Tim theo chu so huu

Tim theo tag nhanh:
- Nhap #<text> trong o tim kiem
- Vi du: #hop_dong

4.8. Yeu thich mau
- Bam icon ngoi sao tai danh sach hoac chi tiet mau
- Mau da danh dau se xuat hien trong muc "Yeu thich"

4.9. Quyen sua/xoa mau
Theo nghiep vu hien tai:
- Mau private: owner sua/xoa
- Mau phong ban: truong nhom cua nhom do sua/xoa
- Mau cong khai: admin sua/xoa

4.10. Upload hang loat mau
Neu co nhieu mau:
- Vao "Upload hang loat"
- Tai file theo dinh dang he thong yeu cau
- Kiem tra tung dong ket qua
- Mo nhanh mau da tao neu co


5. SINH VAN BAN BANG AI

5.1. Chon mau de sinh van ban
Co 2 cach:
- Vao menu "Sinh van ban bang AI"
- Hoac mo chi tiet mau roi bam tao van ban

5.2. Dien thong tin thu cong
Tai man hinh dien bien:
- Dien tieu de van ban
- Dien cac truong tuong ung voi cac bien trong mau

5.3. Dien tu ho so
Chuc nang "Dien tu ho so":
- AI doc thong tin ho so ca nhan cua ban
- Tu dong dien cac truong phu hop

De ket qua tot hon:
- Cap nhat day du thong tin trong Ho so ca nhan

5.4. Dien tu ngu canh cong ty
Chuc nang "Dien tu ngu canh cong ty":
- AI doc thong tin cong ty da duoc cau hinh
- Dien cac truong lien quan nhu ten cong ty, dia chi, don vi...

5.5. Trich xuat tu PDF
Chuc nang "Trich xuat tu PDF":
- Upload file PDF
- He thong trich text
- AI gan noi dung tu PDF vao cac bien phu hop

5.6. Prompt bo tro
Neu he thong co danh sach prompt:
- Ban co the chon prompt de dieu chinh cach AI dien hoac sinh van ban

5.7. Xem truoc va tao van ban
Sau khi dien xong:
- Xem truoc noi dung
- Bam tao van ban
- He thong sinh van ban DOCX va luu vao muc Quan ly van ban

5.8. Timeout va debug
Voi cac chuc nang AI:
- Dien tu ho so
- Dien tu ngu canh cong ty
- Trich xuat tu PDF
- Sinh van ban bang AI

He thong da duoc dat timeout rieng dai hon.
Trong che do debug, console co the hien:
- Thoi gian da xu ly
- Buoc dang thuc hien
- Luong request/response


6. QUAN LY VAN BAN

6.1. Cac nhom van ban
Menu "Quan ly van ban" gom:
- Van ban cua toi
- Da chia se trong nhom
- Da chia se cong khai
- Yeu thich
- Da luu tru
- Tat ca (Admin)

Y nghia:
- Van ban cua toi: van ban private do ban tao
- Da chia se trong nhom: van ban duoc chia se theo nhom va da duyet
- Da chia se cong khai: van ban public da duyet
- Yeu thich: van ban ban da danh dau
- Da luu tru: van ban private cua ban da dua vao luu tru

6.2. Danh sach van ban
Man hinh danh sach ho tro:
- Tim kiem
- Loc nang cao
- Kieu hien thi:
  + Dang card
  + Dang danh sach doc
- Chon nhieu muc bang checkbox
- Xoa hang loat neu co quyen

Co the tim theo:
- Ten van ban
- So hieu van ban
- Ten mau
- Chu so huu
- Ghi chu

6.3. Chi tiet van ban
Khi mo 1 van ban, ban co the xem:
- Noi dung
- File Word neu co
- Trang thai
- Pham vi chia se
- Lich su phien ban
- Thong tin tao/cap nhat

6.4. Tai Word
Neu van ban co file DOCX:
- Bam "Tai Word" de tai ve may

6.5. Chia se van ban
Trong "Cai dat quyen xem", ban co the dat:
- Chi minh toi
- Chia se voi nhom cua toi
- Chia se voi tat ca moi nguoi

Co che phe duyet:
- Chia se nhom:
  + Neu ban la truong nhom hoac admin: co hieu luc ngay
  + Neu khong: cho truong nhom duyet
- Chia se cong khai:
  + Admin co hieu luc ngay
  + Nguoi dung thuong: cho admin duyet

6.6. Luu tru van ban
Luu y quan trong:
- Chi duoc luu tru van ban private cua chinh ban
- Van ban da chia se trong nhom khong duoc luu tru
- Van ban da chia se cong khai khong duoc luu tru

Khi luu tru:
- Van ban se roi khoi muc cu
- Xuat hien trong muc "Da luu tru"

6.7. Khoi phuc van ban da luu tru
Tai chi tiet van ban da luu tru:
- Bam "Khoi phuc"
- Van ban quay ve danh sach van ban dang hoat dong

6.8. Yeu thich van ban
Van ban co che yeu thich tuong tu mau:
- Bam icon sao tai danh sach
- Hoac bam icon sao tai man chi tiet
- Van ban se xuat hien trong muc "Yeu thich"

6.9. Xoa van ban
Quyen xoa:
- Private: owner
- Group: truong nhom cua nhom do
- Public: admin

6.10. Phien ban van ban
He thong luu lich su phien ban:
- Xem danh sach phien ban
- Khoi phuc phien ban cu
- An/hien phien ban


7. CHO PHE DUYET

Muc "Cho phe duyet" hien cho:
- Admin
- Truong nhom

No bao gom 2 loai doi tuong:
- Mau van ban cho phe duyet
- Van ban cho phe duyet chia se

7.1. Phe duyet mau van ban
- Mau phong ban: truong nhom hoac admin duyet theo nghiep vu
- Mau cong khai: admin duyet

7.2. Phe duyet van ban chia se
- Van ban chia se nhom: truong nhom duyet
- Van ban chia se cong khai: admin duyet

7.3. Hanh dong
Tai man hinh nay co the:
- Mo chi tiet doi tuong
- Duyet
- Tu choi


8. CHAT AI VA HOI DAP VAN BAN

8.1. Chat AI
Muc "Chat AI":
- Tro chuyen voi AI theo kieu chat tu do
- Dung de hoi dap nhanh, viet nhap, goi y noi dung

8.2. Hoi dap van ban voi AI
Muc "Hoi dap van ban voi AI":
- Dat cau hoi tren ngu canh du lieu van ban da duoc index
- Dung khi can tim thong tin trong he thong van ban


9. PROMPTS

Muc "Prompts" dung de:
- Quan ly cac prompt dung cho AI
- Tao prompt rieng
- Chia se prompt theo pham vi private/group/public neu he thong cho phep

Prompt co the duoc su dung trong:
- Sinh van ban bang AI
- Cac quy trinh goi mo hinh


10. HO SO CA NHAN

Ho so ca nhan rat quan trong vi du lieu nay duoc dung cho:
- Dien tu ho so
- Hien thi thong tin nguoi dung
- Mot so quy trinh nghiep vu khac

Ban nen cap nhat day du:
- Ho ten
- Chuc danh
- CCCD
- Ngay sinh
- Ma nhan vien
- Bio va cac thong tin bo sung


11. QUAN TRI

11.1. Bang quan tri
Danh cho nhan su co quyen quan tri:
- Quan ly nguoi dung
- Quan ly nhom
- Them thanh vien vao nhom
- Phan vai tro member/leader/admin theo nghiep vu

11.2. Tim nguoi dung khi them thanh vien
O them thanh vien:
- Co o tim kiem
- Nhap ten/username/email
- He thong goi y nguoi dung tuong ung

11.3. Cau hinh AI
Admin co the:
- Chon model
- Cau hinh ket noi AI
- Cau hinh ngu canh cong ty

11.4. Sao luu du lieu
Neu la superuser:
- Tao ban sao luu
- Tai ban sao luu
- Xoa ban sao luu


12. QUY TAC PHAN QUYEN CAN NHO

12.1. Mau van ban
- Private: owner sua/xoa
- Group: truong nhom sua/xoa
- Public: admin sua/xoa

12.2. Van ban
- Private: owner quan ly day du, co the luu tru
- Group: khong duoc luu tru; chia se can leader duyet neu chua du quyen
- Public: khong duoc luu tru; chia se can admin duyet neu chua du quyen

12.3. Phe duyet
- Truong nhom duyet doi tuong lien quan nhom minh
- Admin duyet doi tuong cong khai


13. MEO SU DUNG HIEU QUA

- Dien day du Ho so ca nhan de chuc nang "Dien tu ho so" hoat dong tot
- Neu co tai lieu scan/PDF, dung "Trich xuat tu PDF" de tiet kiem thoi gian nhap tay
- Dung tag cho mau de tim nhanh hon
- Dung #tag khi tim mau van ban
- Dung muc Yeu thich de danh dau mau/van ban dung thuong xuyen
- Dung Dang danh sach doc neu muon xem nhieu muc nhanh hon
- Dung checkbox de xoa hang loat thay vi xoa tung muc


14. CAC LOI THUONG GAP VA HUONG XU LY

14.1. Khong thay mau phong ban hoac mau cong khai
Nguyen nhan co the:
- Mau chua duoc phe duyet
- Chua chon dung nhom dich
- Tai khoan hien tai khong nam trong nhom do

14.2. Khong thay van ban trong "Da luu tru"
Chi nhung van ban private cua ban moi duoc luu tru.
Van ban chia se nhom/cong khai se khong vao muc nay.

14.3. Chuc nang AI xu ly lau
Co the do:
- File lon
- Model AI cham
- PDF kho trich xuat
- Server AI dang ban

Hay doi them va xem debug console neu dang o che do dev.

14.4. Khong chia se duoc
Kiem tra:
- Van ban da luu tru khong duoc chia se
- Chia se nhom can chon nhom dich
- Chia se cong khai co the can admin duyet

14.5. Khong luu tru duoc
Kiem tra:
- Van ban co phai private cua ban khong
- Neu da chia se nhom hoac cong khai thi khong duoc luu tru


15. KHUYEN NGHI VAN HANH

- Thuong xuyen gan tag cho mau
- Luu y pham vi hien thi truoc khi gui phe duyet
- Dung Yeu thich cho cac doi tuong dung nhieu
- Kiem tra ky noi dung va bien truoc khi sinh van ban bang AI
- Admin nen dinh ky sao luu du lieu

2. Dien bien thu cong hoac bang AI
3. Trich xuat PDF neu can

5. Tao van ban

Luong 3: Chia se van ban
1. Mo chi tiet van ban

1. Danh dau yeu thich neu dung thuong xuyen
2. Tai Word khi can
